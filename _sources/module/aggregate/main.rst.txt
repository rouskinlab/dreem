
Aggregation 
=============================

Aggregation turns the bitvectors into a :ref:`dreem_output` file. 

**Key features:**
    - Computing the mutation population average for all bitvectors.
    - Computing the mutation population average for clusters of bitvectors.
    - Predicting the free energy and the structure from the sequence using :ref:`dependencies_rnastructure`.
